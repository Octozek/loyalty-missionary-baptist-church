BAPTISM

What is Baptism?

Baptism is an outward expression of an inward transformation. It symbolizes the washing away of sins and the beginning of a new life in Christ. At our church, we believe baptism is an important step in a believer's journey with God, following the example of Jesus and publicly declaring one's faith.

If you are interested in being baptized, or if you want to learn more about the significance of baptism, please reach out to us. We would love to guide you through this meaningful process.
