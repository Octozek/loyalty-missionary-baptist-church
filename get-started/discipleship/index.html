DISCIPLESHIP

Discipleship at Our Church

Discipleship is at the core of what we believe, as Jesus commands us to go and make disciples of all nations. At our church, we aim to provide resources and guidance to help each believer grow in their faith and follow Christ with commitment. Through study, fellowship, and service, we strive to become true disciples of Jesus Christ.

Join us on this journey as we learn, grow, and serve together, spreading the love and teachings of Jesus in our community and beyond.
