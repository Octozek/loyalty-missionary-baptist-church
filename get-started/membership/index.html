MEMBERSHIP

Membership at Our Church

Becoming a member of our church means joining a family committed to growing together in faith and love. Church membership is more than just attending; it's about being actively involved in the mission and ministry of our community. We believe that every member has a role to play and can contribute to building God's kingdom.

If you are interested in becoming a member, we encourage you to get in touch with us to learn more about the membership process, the responsibilities, and the blessings of being part of a church family.
